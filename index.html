Privacy Policy

Countdown - Widget
Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Countdown - Widget app ("the App", "we", "us") handles information. The App is developed and operated by Jeongim Yu, an independent developer. If you have any questions, contact jiyudeldel@gmail.com.

Summary

  • The countdowns, categories, notes, and photos you create are stored only on your device. We do not collect them or have access to them.
  • The App is free and supported by ads. We use Google AdMob to show ads and Google Firebase to understand app usage and diagnose crashes.
  • We do not sell your personal information, and the App does not require an account.

Information stored on your device

Everything you create in the App — your countdowns, categories, notes, and any photos you choose as card backgrounds — is saved locally on your device (and, for the Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets, in a shared container on your device). This information is not transmitted to us or to any server. It remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

Information collected by third-party services

To keep the App free, we use the following third-party services, which may collect certain information directly from your device:

Google AdMob (advertising). AdMob serves the ads shown in the App. To do this, it may process information such as your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), IP address, general location derived from your IP address, and ad interactions. With your permission (see App Tracking Transparency below), this information may be used to show personalized ads; without permission, you will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.

Google Firebase Analytics (usage analytics). Firebase Analytics collects information about how the App is used — for example, which features are opened and basic device and operating-system information — to help us understand and improve the App. It uses an app-instance identifier rather than your name.

Google Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting). If the App crashes, Crashlytics collects diagnostic information about the crash and your device's state at that moment so we can fix problems.

These Google services process data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn how Google uses information from apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Apple SKAdNetwork. The App uses Apple's SKAdNetwork to measure the effectiveness of advertising in a privacy-preserving way. SKAdNetwork reports aggregated install information to Apple and does not share a cross-app identifier with us.

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, the App asks for your permission before using your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to deliver personalized ads. You can grant or deny this at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. If you deny tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized.

Consent (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App presents a consent message before serving ads, asking whether you agree to the use of your data for advertising purposes. You can change or withdraw your choices at any time through the privacy options available in the App.

Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

Where the General Data Protection Regulation applies, we and our service providers rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for the use of advertising identifiers and personalized advertising, where you have agreed via the consent prompt and ATT.
  • Legitimate interests — for analytics and crash reporting used to maintain and improve the App, in a manner that does not override your rights.

Your rights

EEA / UK / Switzerland (GDPR): you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, the right to data portability, and the right to withdraw consent at any time. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Denmark, this is Datatilsynet (datatilsynet.dk).

California (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jiyudeldel@gmail.com. You can also manage advertising choices on your device through Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, where you can reset your advertising identifier.

Data retention

Information you create stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Information collected by the third-party services above is retained according to their own policies.

Children's privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, contact us at jiyudeldel@gmail.com and we will take appropriate steps.

International data transfers

The third-party services we use are operated by Google LLC and Apple Inc., which may process data in the United States and other countries. These providers apply their own safeguards for international transfers as described in their privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and post the new version on this page.
